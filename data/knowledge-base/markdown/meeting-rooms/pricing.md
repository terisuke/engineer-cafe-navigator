## Pricing Structure

### Time Slots
- **Morning**: 9:00-12:00
- **Afternoon**: 13:00-17:00
- **Evening**: 18:00-21:00
- **Day (Morning + Afternoon)**: 9:00-17:00
- **Afternoon + Evening**: 13:00-21:00
- **Full Day**: 9:00-21:00
